About SWFT Blockchain

SWFT Blockchain is a platform that allows users to trade, invest, and transact in cryptocurrencies. Established in 2017, the company has the backing of renowned investors Draper Dragon and Node Capital. With over 800+ cryptocurrencies available on the platform and access to 50+ public blockchain networks, SWFT Blockchain offers a comprehensive suite of services for crypto users. Additionally, the company is registered as a Money Services Business (MSB), ensuring compliance with financial regulations and providing added security for users' transactions. Overall, SWFT Blockchain is a trusted partner for anyone looking to engage with the world of cryptocurrencies.
